About the course

Hands on. Interactive. Limited.

We enroll limited seats so that we can give focused attention to each candidates. Everyone have their own pace of learning. So, we mentor each of you individually.

Ideally a batch consists of minimum of 6 to maximum of 10 people. We work on 3 to 5 real world projects.

Portfolio driven. Outcome focused.

We work on 3 to 5 real world projects depending on the batch strength. By end of the course each candidate will have a set of decent portfolio to showcase.

The course is completely outcome focused. You will be industry ready and should be able to land on any UX job roles in the market.

1-to-1 Mentorship. 6 Intensive weeks

We meet every saturday to get hands-on training and implement it through the week. There is constant online support through the week. Since we are small, each one of you gets dedicated time and mentorship sessions.

Although the planned sessions are only on weekends, we work intensively throughout the week.

Career assistance. Industry standards.

Each one of us are different. Design industry is diverse and based on your individual competencies, we guide you to appropriate design vertices. Help understand where to find answers. Make you industry ready!

You should have a good collection of portfolio, case studies and equipped to showcase them to the companies.
